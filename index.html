📖 Guide

How to Play

Master the art of coffee-making in a fast-paced tycoon experience. Every second counts when the morning rush hits!

01
👥
Customers Arrive
Customers walk in and queue up, each showing their drink order. They have a patience meter — don't keep them waiting too long!
02
🖱️
Click to Brew
Click the matching drink machine to start brewing. Watch the brew bar fill up — different drinks take different amounts of time.
03
💰
Earn Coins
Successfully serve a customer to earn coins. Chain orders for streak bonuses! Miss too many customers and your rating drops.
04
⬆️
Buy Upgrades
Spend your coins on shop upgrades — faster grinders, soothing music to calm customers, loyalty cards for extra income, and more counters.
✨ What's Inside

Game Features

☕
4 Unique Drinks
Espresso, Latte, Frappuccino & Tea — each with different brew times and rewards.
⚡
4 Shop Upgrades
Turbo Grinder, Cozy Music, Loyalty Cards & Extra Counter — multi-level progression.
🔥
Streak Bonus System
Serve customers back-to-back for combo bonuses. Keep your streak alive for maximum profit.
⏱️
120-Second Shift
A complete game loop that fits in 2 minutes — rush your shift and earn an S-rank grade.
😊
Patience Meter
Dynamic patience bars for each customer — green to red. Upgrades extend patience time.
🏆
End-Shift Grading
S / A / B / C / D ranking based on your serve rate. Stats screen tracks every metric.
